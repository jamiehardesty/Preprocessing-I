Luci from Disenchantment

Meet Luci!

Luci is the deuteragonist in Disenchantment. Jamie's favorite alias of his is "Cat Thing". He lives in Dreamland, is Princess Bean's "personal demon", and is the owner and bartender at Luci's Inferno. He is voiced by Eric Andre.

So... What Does Luci Do?

Luci's behavior is chaotic and seemingly random in places, yet in others, it is pivotal to moving things forward. Luci's actions vary from encouraging worse-than-normal behavior and actively disrupting events to being helpful in a crisis.
