Luci from Disenchantment

Intro to Luci!

Luci is the deuteragonist in Disenchantment. Jamie's favorite alias of his is "Cat Thing". He lives in Dreamland, is Princess Bean's "personal demon", and is the owner and bartender at Luci's Inferno. He is voiced by Eric Andre.

What are Luci's skills?

Luci's behavior is chaotic and seemingly random in places, yet in others, it is pivotal to moving things forward. Luci's actions vary from encouraging worse-than-normal behavior and actively disrupting events to being helpful in a crisis.

Luci's Work History

Luci is currently working on his contract with Beanie. On this project, his managers Cloyd and Becky review Luci's work on Beanie through a mystic green fire. Beanie is free to do as she will, however Luci sends her astray, fulfilling his purpose as a puppet.

Contract Luci!

Luci is unlikely to accept any new contracts. He recently got a promotion from his superiors in Hell and received his wings, however Luci traded his wings, promotion, and immortality to be a good friend to Beanie, in season 2, episode 2, "Starway to Hell".
